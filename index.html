Our Lore

📚 Class 7

It’s actually crazy how long we’ve known each other. Back in class 7 we were just kids trying to survive school, making noise, laughing at the dumbest things, and somehow creating memories that would follow us for years.


None of us knew that the random conversations, the jokes, the chaos, and the friendship would still matter this much years later. But somehow they still do.

🎧 Spotify Era

There was a time when our Spotify playlists basically shared one soul. Same songs. Same late night moods. Same random obsession with tracks that made us feel like movie characters.


Some songs still carry memories so strongly that hearing them feels like opening old messages. Music became part of the friendship, and honestly, I don’t think I’ll ever hear some tracks the same way again.

🌌 Star Gazing

Those nights felt unreal sometimes. Looking at the sky, talking about life, dreams, fears, relationships, and everything in between like the world had paused for a moment.


It’s funny how some of the deepest conversations happen under quiet stars. No pressure. No pretending. Just two people existing in the same moment, trying to understand life together.

📞 Late Night Calls

The late night calls were actually insane. Hours disappearing without even noticing. Talking about random things, serious things, funny things, and somehow still finding more to say.


Looking back now, those calls became part of growing up. Tiny moments that probably seemed normal back then, but now feel like pieces of a time we can never fully recreate again.

🛒 Market Arc

Then came the market shop era. Rita and Tylan pulling up, chilling there, talking, laughing, and turning an ordinary place into another memory spot.


Life kept changing, people kept growing, and things slowly became different, but somehow the memories from those moments still stayed warm. That’s probably what real friendship does.
